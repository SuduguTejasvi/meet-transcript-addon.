‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I start transcription in a Google Meet session?
A: Click the Live Transcript icon in Google Meet, then click "Start Activity" to open the main transcript view, and finally click "Start Live Transcript" to begin real-time transcription.
Q: Why isn't the Live Transcript icon appearing in my Google Meet?
A: Make sure the add-on is properly installed from the Google Workspace Marketplace. Refresh your browser page and check that you have the necessary permissions.
Q: Is my audio data secure and private?
A: Yes, your audio is processed in real-time and is not permanently stored. All data transmission is encrypted, and we follow strict privacy guidelines. See our Privacy Policy for details.
Q: Can I use Live Transcript in any Google Meet session?
A: Live Transcript works in most Google Meet sessions, but requires proper permissions and a stable internet connection. Some organizational policies may restrict usage.
Q: What browsers are supported?
A: Live Transcript works with Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest versions). Ensure your browser allows microphone access for optimal functionality.
Q: How accurate is the transcription?
A: Transcription accuracy depends on audio quality, speaker clarity, and background noise. We use advanced AI technology (Deepgram) for high-quality results, typically achieving 90%+ accuracy in good conditions.

Common Issues & Solutions

Installation Problems

  • Issue: Cannot install from marketplace
  • Solution: Check Google Workspace admin permissions and marketplace access

Audio Access Issues

  • Issue: No audio being captured
  • Solution: Check browser microphone permissions and ensure microphone is not muted

Transcription Not Starting

  • Issue: Transcription button not working
  • Solution: Verify internet connection and refresh the page

Poor Transcription Quality

  • Issue: Inaccurate or garbled text
  • Solution: Check audio quality, reduce background noise, ensure clear speech

Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 27, 2024

Important: Live Transcript processes audio data in real-time for transcription purposes. We do not store your audio data permanently, and all processing is done securely through encrypted connections.

1. Information We Collect

Live Transcript collects and processes the following types of information:

  • Audio Data: Real-time audio streams from Google Meet sessions for transcription purposes
  • Participant Information: Names and identifiers of meeting participants (as provided by Google Meet)
  • Meeting Metadata: Meeting IDs and basic session information
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the add-on (features used, frequency of use)

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Transcription Services: Converting speech to text in real-time
  • Participant Identification: Mapping audio streams to participant names
  • Service Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns to improve the service
  • Technical Support: Providing assistance and troubleshooting

3. Data Processing and Storage

Real-time Processing: Audio data is processed in real-time and is not permanently stored on our servers.

Third-party Services: We use the following services for processing:

  • Deepgram: For speech-to-text conversion (subject to Deepgram's privacy policy)
  • Google Cloud Platform: For authentication and API access
  • Google Meet: For audio stream access (subject to Google's privacy policy)

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • All audio data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Authentication is handled through secure OAuth 2.0 protocols
  • No audio data is stored on our servers
  • Access to participant information is limited to necessary personnel

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties except:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • With your explicit consent
  • To third-party service providers who assist in operating our service (under strict confidentiality agreements)

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request information about what data we have about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Stop using the service at any time

7. Cookies and Tracking

Live Transcript may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze usage patterns and improve the service
  • Provide personalized experiences

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

8. Children's Privacy

Live Transcript is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
